The name Silicon Hills derives from the fact that in the 90s many Tech and Computer came to Austin. Austin shot up in a matter of a decade to become the 11th largest city with in the country. Companies like DELL,Google and Apple have offices here. Although Austin might not be known for being big Austin had went from your average town to a sprawling city infused with life and Tech in a matter of years. DELL was founded by Michael Dell when he was a student at the University of Texas Austin campus. His creation of DELL corporation helped bring Austin to the type of city it is today.
