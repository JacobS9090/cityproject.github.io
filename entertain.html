Austin is a City filled with entertainment!

Sports

Austin is a city filled with stuff to do and things to see. Austin is home to several sports teams such as the Texas Longhorns who are the college team from the University of Texas in Austin. Austin is also home to the annual DELL WGC tournament which brings in golfers from around the world. Texas is also a home to Motorsports as well as Austin is home to the Circuit of the Americas Formula 1 track which was opened in 2012. The Austin sports scene does not consist of much as cities like Dallas,Houston and San Antonio are larger with notable sports teams.

SXSW and Music

Austin is also known for some of it's conventions and live music as well. Due to a large Hispanic population Spanish music plays it's part in this town. Austin is also home to lots of live music venues mostly of Blues and Country as well as Indie and Pop. The Austin music scene began in the 1960s during the Psychadelic Sound Era but also in the 1950s where East Austin was a home for Blues and Jazz. The music scene for Austin only continues to grow. The SXSW convention also happens here for mostly week bringing music as well film and other forms of entertainment plus speakers who speak out on different topics.
