Tex-Mex is not really Mexican food but is more or less like it's American cousin. Tex Mex unlike Mexican uses hard-shell Taco shells and Yellow Cheese. Tex Mex like unlike Mexican does not use fresh beans but instead uses canned. Tex Mex also uses sweet corn as well as the use of the spice Cumin. Tex-Mex originates from the Mexican people living within the state though got very popular after the establishment of international transit from trains coming from Mexico to cities like Austin which was big in railway trade as well as El Paso and San Antonio.
